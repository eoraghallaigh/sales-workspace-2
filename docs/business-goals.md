## North star

A single workspace to view and engage all of a rep's prospects.

## Strategic context (from the BoB PRD)

- **Flywheel OGP:** Scale Downmarket — Automate SB Prospecting
- **Driver:** reduce CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) for SMB by automating prospecting and capacity management
- **Launch moment:** Feb 1, 2026 — global BoB (Book of Business) operating model rollout for SMB

## Current OKRs

### Objective 1: Make it easier for reps to view their most important prospects
- **KR1:** Reps work more of their prospect accounts

### Objective 2: Reps work their prospect accounts more deeply and thoroughly 
- **KR1:** Reps reach out to more contacts per account
- **KR2:** Reps make more outreach engagements per contact

## Primary metrics we move

- Avg contacts worked per account
- P1 to P4 SLAs met
- - P1 SLA = Reach out within 24hrs of assignment, reach out 5 times in 14 days.
  - P2 SLA = Reach out 10 times in rolling 90 day window
  - P3 SLA = Reach out within 24hrs, reach out 4 times in 14 days.
  - P4 SLA = None
